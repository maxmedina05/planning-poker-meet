2. Description of the service

Planning Poker runs as a side-panel add-on within Google Meet. It lets meeting participants vote on story point estimates using a standard Fibonacci-style card deck (0, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21, ?, and a coffee break card). The first participant to open the panel becomes the facilitator and can set a story title, reveal votes, and start new rounds. Any participant can claim the facilitator role if the original facilitator leaves.

The add-on is provided free of charge for personal and professional use. There are no paid tiers, subscriptions, or premium features.
